PRAWN SUKKA MASALA


Prawn Sukka is a dish from the west coast of South India.It is a bright orange(red in some cases) spicy and Tangy prawn roast made with fresh coconut and dry red chillies.


  • Prep Time: 10 Mins
  • Cook Time: 45Mins
  • Servings :4 persons

Ingredients:


  • Prawns - 1/2 kg
  • Coconut oil - 2 tbsp
  • Cinnamon - a piece
  • Few Cloves
  • Cardamom pods - 2 nos.
  • Fennel Seeds - 1/4 tsp
  • Onions - 2 nos (finely chopped)
  • Ginger Garlic paste - 1 tsp
  • Green Chillies - 2 nos (slit)
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Chili powder - 2 tsp
  • Puree of 2 Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Few Curry Leaves
  • Coriander powder - 2 tsp

Method:


  1. Heat oil. Add Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom & Fennel Seeds.

  2. Add onions and saute till golden brown

  3. Add ginger garlic paste, green chilli, turmeric powder, chili powder, tomato puree & salt

  4. Cook till it thicken then reduce flame

  5. Add the prawns and curry leaves and cook till its 3/4th done

  6. Add coriander powder and mix. Increase flame and cook till the masala thickens. Done!
