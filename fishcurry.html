INDIAN FISH CURRY


This spicy dish is a scintillating stew of curry cooked with vegetables and of course, the whole head of a fish. This delectable dish is usually cooked with both tamarind and coconut milk, giving it a rich, nuanced flavour.


  • Prep Time: 30 Mins
  • Cook Time: 50 mins
  • Servings: 4 persons

Ingredients:


  • Fish- 4 medium-big pieces
  • Oil - 2 Tbsp
  • Fenugreek seeds - a pinch
  • Garlic cloves - few (pounded)
  • Shallots - 7 to 8 nos
  • Green Chilis - 3 nos
  • Tamarind puree - 2 to 3 Tbsp (diluted)
  • Chili powder - 2 Tbsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Few Curry leaves
  • Tomato - 1 no
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt
  • water
  • Few Coriander leaves

Method:


  1. In a wide deep pan, heat oil. Add fenugreek seeds, pounded garlic and shallots & green chilis. saute till transparent

  2. Mean while, Dilute tamarind puree in a bowl. Add chili powder and coriander powder. Mix well

  3. Once the onion turns transparent, add curry leaves, tomato, turmeric powder & salt and Mix well.

  4. Add the tamarind chili powder sauce & some water and Mix well.

  5. Close & cook for 15 to 20 minutes

  6. Now add the fish pieces. Close & cook for 2 to 3 minues.

  7. Turn the pieces, add curry leaves & coriander leaves. Close & cook again for 2 to 3 minutes. Done!
