CHICKEN 65


Chicken 65 is a spicy, deep-fried chicken dish originating from Hotel Buhari, Chennai, India, as an entrée, or quick snack. The flavour of the dish can be attributed to red chillies, but the exact set of ingredients for the recipe can vary.


  • Prep Time: 30 Mins
  • Cook Time:30mins
  • Servings: 3 persons

Ingredients:


  • Chicken - 1/2 kg
  • Chilli powder - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Ginger & garlic paste - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Curd/Yogurt - 2 tsp
  • Lemon juice - 2 tsp
  • rice flour- 1 1/2 tbsp
  • Oil for frying


Method:


  1. The key step to this recipe is marinating. To the Chicken add Chilli powder, Turmeric powder, Ginger & garlic paste, Salt, Curd/yogurt, Lemon juice & mix it well.

  2. Let it marinate for about an hour.

  3. Before to start frying add rice flour to coat the chicken pieces.

  4. Once Oil is hot, gently drop the marinated chicken pieces & fry until it turns golden colour.

  5. Serve hot with Biryani.
