CURD RICE


Curd rice, also called yogurt rice, is a dish originating from India. The word "curd" in Indian English refers to unsweetened probiotic yogurt. It is most popular in the South India.


  • Prep Time: 10 Mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Servings: 2 persons

Ingredients:


  • Cooked Rice - 2 cups
  • Yogurt/Curd - 1 cup (cold)
  • Milk - 1/2 cup (cold/room temperature)
  • Oil - 1 tsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • Chana dal - 1/2 tsp
  • Urad dal - 1/2 tsp
  • 4 tbsp Lime juice
  • Small piece Ginger - finely chopped
  • Green chili - 1 no. finely chopped
  • Few Curry leaves

Method:


  1. Take 2 cups of cooked rice. Let it cool down. Mash it lightly.

  2. To the rice, add the curd (preferred cold) and mix well

  3. Add milk (preferred cold) and mix, then salt (optional)

  4. Now temper mustard seeds, chana dal, urad dal, ginger, green chili and curry leaves in little oil

  5. Add the tempered ingredients to the rice and mix (tempering is optional)

  6. Garnish the curd rice with coriander leaves and pomegranate
