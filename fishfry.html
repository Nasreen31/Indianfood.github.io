SPICY FISH FRY


Indian fish fry recipe is so flavorful and delicious with spices. It is an easy and simple fried fish recipe to make in just a few minutes. In Indian cuisine, all the seafood recipes include spices like turmeric and coriander powder that helps to reduce the stinky smell from the fish and to add flavor and aroma to the dishes.


  • Prep Time: 30 Mins
  • Cook Time: 30 Hour
  • Servings :3 persons

Ingredients:


  • Small Whole Fish - 4 Nos
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 2 Tsp
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 Tsp
  • Chilli Powder - 4 Tsp
  • Salt - 1 Tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 2 Tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1 Tsp
  • Pepper - 1
  • corn flour - 2 1/2 Tsp
  • Juice Of 1/2 Lemon
  • Water
  • Oil For Frying
  • Onion - 1 No. Thinly Sliced
  • Curry Leaves

Method:


  1. For marinade: In a bowl add red chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder,cumin powder,pepper powder, salt and mix well.

  2. Add lemon juice and mix well.

  3. Add water little by little and make a smooth paste.

  4. Apply the marinade evenly on the fish. Marinate in the refrigerator for 20 minutes and then add corn flour on it.

  5. Add oil in a pan and place marinated fish in the hot oil carefully. Fry until the bottom side of the fish becomes crispy.

  6. Flip gently and fry on the other side until crispy.

  7. Line a plate with paper towel. Remove the fried fish from the oil and place on the paper towel-lined plate (this helps to absorb the excess oil from fish). Garnish with sliced onion and lemon if desired and serve.
