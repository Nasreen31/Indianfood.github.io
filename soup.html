GOAT LEG SOUP


Paya soup or bone broth is made from leg bones and hoofs of goat or lamb. It is rich in nutrients and minerals and is also considered an immunity booster.Calcium, phosphorus, potassium, and various trace elements and minerals are abundant in paya soup. Additionally, the bone marrow of the Mutton Paya is rich in Omega Fatty acids 3 and 6 in addition to Vitamin A, K, and even a trace amount of selenium.

  • Prep Time: 30 Mins
  • Cook Time: 50 mins
  • Servings: 4 persons

Ingredients:


  • Oil - 1 Tsp
  • Cinnamon
  • Clove
  • Fennel seeds - 1/2 Tsp
  • Bay leaf
  • Goat leg 4 pieces
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Tomato - 1 no.
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 Tsp
  • Rock Salt
  • Curry leaves
  • Water - 3 cups
  • Coriander leaves

    • For masala powder


  • Cumin seeds - 1 Tsp
  • Peppercorn - 1 Tsp
  • Coriander seeds - 1 Tbsp


Method:


  1. In a mixer jar, add cumin seeds, peppercorns, coriander seeds.

  2. Grind it to a fine powder.

  3. Pound some garlic with the skin and keep it aside.
  4. Pound some shallots roughly and keep it aside.

  5. In a pressure cooker, heat some oil, add cinnamon, cloves, fennel seeds, bay leaf, goat legs, pound garlic, pound shallots and Saute them.

  6. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, ground masala powder. rock salt, curry leaves and Mix everything well.

  7. Add 250 ml of water and mix well.

  8. Close the pressure cooker and cook it for about 30 minutes.

  9. Finally, open the pressure cooker and add coriander leaves and curry leaves.

  10. Serve the soup hot with a dash of pepper.
